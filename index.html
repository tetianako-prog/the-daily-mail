About

I’M ANDREW FOSTER AND THIS IS THE DAILY MALE

The Daily Male is run by Andrew Foster – men’s style, lifestyle and grooming blogger and YouTube creator.

My blog and accompanying YouTube channel are the home to everything I explore in men’s style, lifestyle and grooming. From hair tutorials; product reviews; seasonal lookbooks and hauls; to restaurant reviews and cocktail recipes; I shoot videos, take pictures and write about the stuff that makes me tick. I’m particularly interested in the evolving landscape of men’s style and grooming. We’re at a point where men have so many options and we can truly explore our own sense of style, and the kind of products and experiences that work for us. I’m not the oracle, but with years of experience, visual flare and insatiable appetite for discovering new things, I’m confident that you’ll gain a little something from my content.
